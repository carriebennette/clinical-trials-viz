A Decade of Drug Approvals

The 2010s was a ground-breaking decade for the life science industry with the Food & Drug Association approving a large number of innovative new therapies. Here is a visualization of every New Molecular Entity approved, with larger circles representing a greater number of patients included in the clinical trial(s) supporting approval.

Hover over any circle to see more details.
